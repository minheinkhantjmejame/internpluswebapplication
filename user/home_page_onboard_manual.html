AFTER ORIENTATION
Submit your morning report via the report page on the left.
Read the rules and guidelines for intern work on the rules and policies page on the left.
Accept the conditions and practices.
Upload a copy of your ID card and Kasikorn bank account.
ASSIGNMENT SHEET

Assignments or work records must be completed and submitted at the end of each workday.

How to Fill In
In the Tasks/Day column, enter details of the work performed.
In the Time column, enter the work start and end times for each task.
In the Status column, select the status of the task.
WRITING FEEDBACK AFTER INTERNSHIP

When students complete their internship for 1 week or 1 month, write feedback about how the internship went.

The Questions
What did you get from your internship in the past 1 week or 1 month?
In what areas have they developed their skills?
To what extent the internship is as expected?
GUIDELINES FOR EVALUATING WORK DURING INTERNSHIP
Evaluation Criteria
Time to work
Punctuality in submitting work
Accuracy of work
Cooperation in group work
Efficiency in both spoken and written communication
Ability to think analytically and solve problems
REPORTING FOR WORK

Submitting reports is divided into 3 sections. The time period is as follows:

Morning report before starting work.
Before lunch report and update work done in the morning.
Send assignments after work.
TIMESHEET SHEET

Filling out a timesheet records the start and end times of work each day and submit a timesheet every week.

How to Fill In
In the Time In column, enter your attendance time.
In the Time Out column, enter your time off work.
In the case of working from home, specify the site name “Work from home”.
In the case of working at a company, specify the site name "Office".
LEAVE

Leave must be submitted to the supervisor 15 days in advance. After notifying the supervisor, submit the leave form on the leave page on the left.

*Frequent absences and unannounced absences give effect to performance evaluations.
