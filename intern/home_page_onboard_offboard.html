OFFBOARD INTERNSHIP AGREEMENT BY VANNESS PLUS

As per the agreement on maintaining confidentiality prior to the internship, the intern has now completed the internship within the stipulated period. The following agreement is made between the company and the intern to serve as guidelines for conduct and to avoid issues after the completion of the internship:

  1. Works created by the intern during the internship period are considered as the property of Vaness Plus Consulting Co., Ltd. Use of these works beyond the internship is not permitted. If violated, the company may take legal action.
  2. The intern may present their work to their advisor for evaluation purposes, only in the sections permitted by the company.
  3. If the intern needs to present the internship results to the university, you must obtain written permission from the company for what can be disclosed.
  4. You must deliver all work produced during the internship, including creating any manuals related to the assigned work, and provide the company with full access to all work.
  5. The intern has the right to request an internship evaluation form and a certificate of completion.
  6. Whether or not the student's work will be used further depends on the success and results of the work, which is at the sole discretion of the company.
  7. As agreed before the internship, if the student is unable to achieve the internship objectives set by the company, the intern agrees not to receive any allowance or compensation.
  8. The intern will not disclose any information related to the company that they have learned during the internship unless authorized by the company in written document.

The intern hereby signs to consent and acknowledges understanding of the rules and guidelines to be followed and avoided.
